Listing is free during our launch period. When you sell, it's a flat $35 listing fee with no commission — you keep 100% of your sale price and close directly with your buyer through a licensed closing company. When you rent, we take a 3% fee ($59 minimum) from the transaction when it books. Stripe payment processing fees (2.9% + $0.30) are passed through transparently to the renter or buyer at checkout — we don't retain any of it. After the initial 90-day launch period, selling a timeshare is a flat $35 per listing, and renting is covered by a $10/year membership.
