Timeshare Rental Agreement

This Rental Agreement is between Daniel Reyes (“Owner”) and Jordan Mitchell (“Renter”), in connection with a rental facilitated by Indigo Property Services LLC d/b/a ResortFlips (“ResortFlips”). ResortFlips provides the marketplace platform and is not a party to this Agreement, nor a real estate broker, escrow agent, or insurer.

1. The Rental

Owner agrees to provide, and Renter agrees to rent, the following: Marriott's Aruba Ocean Club — 2BR Ocean Front, for the Rental Period Mar 14 – Mar 21, 2026 (all dates Eastern Time), under Resort confirmation number MAR-80421-50293, for a total Rental Payment of $2,695 plus a pass-through payment-processing fee disclosed at checkout.

2. Verification & Payment Protection

Before Renter's payment is captured, ResortFlips confirms the reservation directly with the Resort. Renter's payment is then held by ResortFlips's payment processor and released to Owner only 24–48 hours after Renter checks in — protecting Renter against a reservation that is not honored at the front desk. If the reservation is not honored through no fault of Renter, Renter receives a full refund.

3. Name on Reservation

Owner shall change the name on the reservation to Renter's name within forty-eight (48) hours of this Agreement taking effect, and forward updated confirmation to ResortFlips. Renter is encouraged to confirm with the Resort that Renter's name appears on the reservation before travel. Owner shall not list, rent, or transfer the same reservation through any other channel.

4. Renter Obligations

Renter will comply with all Resort rules (occupancy limits, pet/smoking, noise, check-in/out times), is responsible for resort fees, lodging taxes, deposits, and optional-service charges imposed by the Resort, and for any damage beyond ordinary wear. Renter may not sublease or assign this Agreement without written consent of Owner and ResortFlips. On-site management may assign the specific physical unit at check-in provided it conforms to the type described above.

5. Owner Obligations

Owner represents the reservation is valid, in Owner's name (or Owner is authorized to designate Renter as occupant), corresponds to the dates and unit described, and that Owner is current on all maintenance fees and dues. Owner will not cancel or allow the reservation to lapse after this Agreement takes effect; if Owner does so, Owner is liable for Renter's and ResortFlips's costs of comparable re-accommodation.

6. Cancellation

Unless a more lenient policy is stated in the listing: a Renter cancellation 65+ days before check-in forfeits 50% of the Rental Payment to Owner; fewer than 65 days forfeits 100%. All cancellations must be processed through the ResortFlips platform.

7. Acknowledgment

By signing, Owner and Renter acknowledge they have read and agreed to this Rental Agreement and the incorporated ResortFlips Terms & Conditions, including the Limitation of Liability, Arbitration and Class-Action Waiver, Disclaimer of Warranties, Cancellation Policy, and the funds-held-until-check-in payment process. The parties agree this Agreement may be signed electronically, and that acceptance through the ResortFlips platform is a valid and binding signature under the E-SIGN Act.
