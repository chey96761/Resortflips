How to read this chart. The numbers shown are full-week points needed to book one week in that villa size/view at that resort during that travel period. Disney's chart uses Sun–Thu and Fri–Sat splits (formula: weekly = 5 × Sun–Thu + 2 × Fri–Sat). Aulani uses uniform Sun–Sat pricing instead.

Travel periods. Most DVC resorts use 7 travel periods. Disneyland Hotel Villas and Animal Kingdom Villas also use 7. Aulani and Grand Californian use 4. Hilton Head uses 4. Vero Beach uses 5.

View categories. Codes vary by resort. Common: S=Standard, P=Preferred, R=Resort, V=Value, TP=Theme Park, PM=Premium (lagoon), C=Concierge, O=Ocean View, SV=Savanna View. Click any resort card for the full breakdown.

Source. Official 2027 Disney Vacation Club chart PDFs from cdn1.parksmedia.wdprapps.disney.com, audit-verified June 2026. Every value cross-checked against Sun–Thu × 5 + Fri–Sat × 2 arithmetic.
