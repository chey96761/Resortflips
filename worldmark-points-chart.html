Key Facts

What makes WorldMark different.

Non-Deeded Ownership

Unlike most timeshare brands, WorldMark uses a non-deeded credit pool. There's no "home resort" or fixed week — you just own annual credits good at all 88 resorts.

Fixed Credit Values

Once a suite is registered into WorldMark, its credit cost is set and cannot change. Owners are protected from inflation in points required.

Operated By

Travel + Leisure Co. (NYSE: TNL), parent of Wyndham Destinations. WorldMark, The Club is a separate company from WorldMark Asia Pacific, but they share a reservation system.

Resale Access

Resale credits can be used at all WorldMark resorts but are NOT eligible for Wyndham Club Pass exchanges (developer-purchased credits only).
