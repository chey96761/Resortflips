How to read these results.

StarOptions are the universal booking currency inside the Vistana Signature Network. Owners of any VSN-eligible Westin or Sheraton week receive a yearly StarOptions deposit, then spend that balance against the published per-resort, per-villa, per-season values to book wherever they want.

This page flips the chart on its head. Instead of asking "how many StarOptions does Week 25 at Nanea cost," you ask the question owners actually have: "what can I do with the points I already have?"

The three affordability bands

  • Comfortable fit — full 7-night Friday-arrival stays where the weekly cost is at or below your balance. No banking, no borrowing.
  • Stretch options — stays where the weekly cost is up to 10% above your balance. Often achievable with a Mon–Thu or Sun–Thu reservation instead of a full week, since weekday nights are roughly 50–65% cheaper than Fri/Sat.
  • Shoulder-season picks — high-end resorts that look out of reach in peak season but fall inside your budget during Silver or Gold weeks (shoulder dates).

What's not shown

Event Weeks, Ultra Premium Weeks, and Specific Weeks at certain resorts may not be bookable at the published StarOptions values shown in the official chart. Home-resort priority rules and some Bella Phase exceptions at Sheraton Vistana Villages can also shift the weeks available in each tier. The lookup uses the published 2020-and-forward VSN values for non-home-resort access — the values most owners actually use.

Sample searches that bring people here

  • "81,000 StarOptions — what can I book?" → Hawaii 1BR Premium, Maui or Kauaʻi, year-round.
  • "148,100 StarOptions — Hawaii?" → 2BR Lockoff at Nanea (Resort View), Kāʻanapali, Princeville, or Kauaʻi — Platinum Plus, all 52 weeks.
  • "95,700 StarOptions — Caribbean?" → 1BR Premium at St. John during shoulder season; or 2BR at Westin Riverfront, Avon.
  • "257,700 StarOptions — best use?" → 3BR Westin St. John (Virgin Grand or Bay Vista) in peak; 3BR Mountain Side at Steamboat in ski-Platinum; or the 3BR Oceanfront at Nanea, anytime.
